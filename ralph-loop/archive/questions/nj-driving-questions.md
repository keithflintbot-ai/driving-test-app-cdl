## Question 1: Traffic Signs

**What does an octagonal red sign indicate?**

A) Stop completely before proceeding
B) Yield to other traffic
C) Road work ahead

**Correct Answer:** A) Stop completely before proceeding

**Explanation:** An octagon shape is used exclusively for stop signs, requiring drivers to come to a complete stop before proceeding.
